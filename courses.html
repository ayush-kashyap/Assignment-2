Undergraduate Courses

Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)

Bachelors in Computer Application (BCA) is a three-year undergraduate degree course for students who wish to delve into the world of Computer languages. The BCA course is one of the most popular options to get started with a career in Information Technology.
Eligibility 12th passed from a recognized board / university.
Duration 3 years
Available seats 80 *
Syllabus Click to view detailed syllabus.

Diploma in Computer Applications

Diploma in Computer Applications is a one-year diploma program that aims to make students study computer applications in depth in a shorter period of time. The DCA program imparts scientific, practical and technical knowledge to its learners about various computer tools that are used in day to day life.
Eligibility 10+2 from a recognized board / university.
Duration 1 year
Available seats 25-30
Syllabus Semester 1:

IT Tools and Business Systems/ Internet Technology and Web Design.

Semester 2:

Programming and Problem Solving Through 'C'/ Introduction to Multimedia, Application of .Net Technology,
Introduction to ICT Resources
