Library

The mission of the Library is to provide information services and access to full text both in digital and printed resources to support the scholarly and information needs of all students, faculty members and staff and to facilitate creation of new knowledge. The mission of the Library is to provide information services and access to full text both in digital and printed resources to support the scholarly and information needs of all students, faculty members and staff and to facilitate creation of new knowledge.

The individual Centers of IPS have their own library. Each library exclusively developed an excellent collection of documents in area of Food Technology, Nutritional Sciences, Media Studies, Computer Science and Fashion Design Technology. The libraries are well equipped with modern facilities and resources (Print and Electronic) in the forms of books, journals, standards, theses, reports, etc. online databases and repositories like INFLIBNET INDEST, DELNET, ETC. through which free online access is provided to majority of the research journals within the premises.
